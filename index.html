02 — About DMR

A single source
for the modern
Maltese build.

DMR Construction is a Malta-based supplier built around a simple idea — a contractor shouldn't need to talk to seven different vendors to finish one project. We stock the materials that go into the structure, the surfaces and the finishes, under one roof.

Our range is built around proven European and regional manufacturers, including Alçıbay, Fullboard, Permolit Boya and Burak Galvaniz — brands with deep technical heritage in drywall, paint and galvanised steel systems.
